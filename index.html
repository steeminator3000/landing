How Liquidao Works

The Liquidao process is straightforward and easy to use.

1

Deposit Your veNFT

Connect your wallet and deposit your time-locked vested NFT into the Liquidao pool.

2

Receive Liquid Tokens

Instantly receive liquid wrapper tokens representing your underlying tokens, minus a small premium based on remaining lock time.

3

Trade or Stake

Use your liquid tokens freely in the DeFi ecosystems - trade them on DEXs or stake them to earn a share of governance voting rewards.

4

Claim Rewards

Stake your liquid tokens to receive voting rewards generated by the community veNFT pool, distributed automatically to all stakers.

async function
liquidifyVNFT(vNFT, account) {
  // Approve the vNFT for transfer
  await vNFT.approve(LIQUIDAO_ADDRESS, tokenId);

  // Calculate the premium based on lock time
  const lockTimeRemaining = await vNFT.getLockTimeRemaining(tokenId);
  const premium = calculatePremium(lockTimeRemaining);

  // Deposit the veNFT and receive liquid tokens
  const liquidTokens = await Liquidao.deposit(tokenId, premium);
  return liquidTokens;
}
